Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

At nnnclothify.shop, we are committed to delivering your order safely and as quickly as possible. Below you'll find everything you need to know about our shipping process.

Shipping Locations

We currently deliver across India (PAN India) and to selected international destinations. If your location is serviceable, we'll do our best to ensure your order reaches you promptly.

Order Processing & Delivery

Order Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 2 business days after confirmation.

Estimated Delivery Time

  • Within India: 2–4 business days
  • International Orders: Delivery times vary depending on the destination and courier services.

Please note that orders are not processed or dispatched on Sundays and public holidays.

While we strive to deliver every order on time, occasional delays may occur due to weather conditions, courier service disruptions, or other unforeseen circumstances. If this happens, we will keep you informed.

Shipping Charges

We offer affordable shipping across our service areas.

  • Shipping charges start from ₹30.
  • The final shipping cost is calculated automatically during checkout based on your delivery location.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been packed and dispatched, we will send you the shipment tracking details through your preferred contact method, allowing you to monitor your delivery status.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions regarding your shipment or delivery, please feel free to contact our customer support team. We're always happy to help.

Thank you for choosing nnnclothify.shop. We truly appreciate your trust and support, and we look forward to serving you again.
